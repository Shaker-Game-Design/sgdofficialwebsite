Shaker Game Design team have following goals:

  1. Enjoy!

    • Our goal is to work as a collaborative team to create our very own game from the ground up. However, the main goal is to have fun; we are making a game after all! Once our game is stable, we plan to release an alpha version to the popular game website, Steam.

  2. Productive!

    • While our main goal is to have fun, it is also important that we are productive as a club. We have three sub-teams: coding, design, and concept. Through specializing in certain jobs, we have the opportunity to work much more efficiently and productively.

  3. Donate!

      4. Part of the profits that we eventually earn from releasing our game will be donated. It is important that we give back to others!
